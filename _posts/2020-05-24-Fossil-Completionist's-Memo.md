## Solution

$$
\begin{align}
F_Q(x) 
&= \mathbb{P}(Q \leq x) \\
&= \mathbb{P}(max(P_1, P_2, ..., P_N) \leq x) \\
&= \mathbb{P}(P_1 \leq x \ ∩ \ P_2 \leq x \ ∩ \ ... \ ∩ \ P_N \leq x) \\
&= \mathbb{P}(P_1 \leq x) \mathbb{P}(P_2 \leq x) \ ... \ \mathbb{P}(P_N \leq x) \\
&= G^{N}(x) \\
\end{align}
$$
